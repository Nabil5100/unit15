web developer

A web developer is a professional who specializes in designing, developing, and maintaining websites. They are responsible for creating the visual appearance and the underlying structure of a website, using a combination of programming languages, frameworks, and design tools. Web developers work closely with clients, project managers, and other team members to understand the requirements and objectives of a website project. They use their technical expertise and creative skills to create a website that meets the needs of the client and the intended audience. Web developers may work on different aspects of a website, such as front-end development or back-end development. Front-end developers focus on creating the user-facing portion of a website, which includes the design, layout, and functionality of the website's pages. They use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create interactive and responsive web pages that look great on different devices

A Managing Director (MD) is the highest-ranking executive in a company or organization, responsible for the overall management and performance of the organization. The MD is typically appointed by the board of directors and is responsible for implementing the strategic vision and goals of the organization, as well as ensuring the organization is financially sound, meeting its targets and goals, and operating effectively and efficiently. The role of a Managing Director varies depending on the size and complexity of the organization, but in general, their responsibilities can include the following: Strategic Planning: The MD is responsible for developing and implementing the organization's strategic plan. This involves setting goals and objectives for the organization, identifying growth opportunities, and developing plans to achieve those goals. Financial Management: The MD oversees the financial management of the organization, ensuring that the organization is financially sound, and that its finances are well-managed. This includes managing budgets, financial reporting, and ensuring that the organization is in compliance with relevant financial regulations and standards.
