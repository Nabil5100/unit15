Job Specification

 

 

Additional Details:

 Working Conditions: The Web Developer will work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, often working on multiple projects at once. The role requires flexibility and adaptability to changing project requirements and timelines. The Web Developer will be required to communicate effectively with project managers, clients, and other team members, both verbally and in writing. The role may involve occasional travel to meet with clients or attend industry events.

Benefits: We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including medical, dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) retirement plan with employer match, paid time off, and paid holidays. We also offer flexible work schedules and a collaborative work environment that values teamwork and innovation. Company  

 

 

 

 Job Overview: 

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Web Developer to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining websites for our clients. The role requires a strong understanding of web development technologies, including front-end and back-end development, as well as the ability to work collaboratively with other team members.

Key Responsibilities:

 Develop and maintain websites using front-end and back-end development technologies. Create responsive web pages that function on multiple devices. Work with project managers and clients to gather requirements and understand project objectives. Collaborate with other team members, including designers and back-end developers, to ensure the website meets the project requirements and objectives. Ensure the website is optimized for search engines and meets accessibility standards. Troubleshoot and resolve website issues, including browser compatibility and website speed. Keep up-to-date with new web development technologies and trends. 

 

 

 

 

 

Qualifications: 

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or related field. Strong understanding of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and at least one back-end programming language (such as PHP or Ruby). Experience with at least one web development framework (such as React, Angular, or Vue.js). Knowledge of web accessibility and SEO best practices. Ability to work collaboratively with other team members and communicate effectively with project managers and clients. Attention to detail and ability to troubleshoot and resolve issues 

Experience: 

At least 2 years of experience in web development. Experience developing websites for clients in a professional environment. Portfolio of web development projects. We offer competitive salary packages, comprehensive benefits, and a collaborative work environment. If you are a talented and motivated web developer looking for a challenging and rewarding opportunity, please apply today.
